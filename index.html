Season 1 - FTB Presents Direwolf20 1.12

Welcome to the brand new website for BlueShell MC! What started out as two friends having fun transformed into a community of players coming together to play Minecraft. With the introduction of this new webpage, I can provide a greater experience for our members. Here, we will achieve greater technical support and be able to make important announcements more visible to members on desktop, tablet, and mobile platforms! Expect to see blog posts, ideas, member spotlights, and server highlights (Be prepared for random checks on all of your progress!) That being said, please provide me any feedback via the Discord; perhaps I will soon make a suggestions page.

Season 1

It wasn't until later on in the maturity of the server, I realized I wanted to have multiple seasons. That's cool and all, but.. why? What is the point? Why the Direwolf20 pack over everything else? I wanted to make sure that we included players of varying experience in the server. The Direwolf20 modpack is what is known as a kitchen sink pack, essentially meaning, we can all learn FTB before moving on to the "I want to down 1750ml of Listerine, this is so difficult" packs.

So what's next?

Weren't you paying attention to anything I just said?!? Well, let's hope you are ready for some direction in your life... Because we are headed for.. drumroll please. FEED THE BEAST AGE OF ENGINEERING 1.12!. That's right, you all asked for it! What does this mean, Joe?? This means you will be given goals by the modpack. There are AGES... yup, AGES. First, you must conquer the Stone Age! You will then progress through the Industrial Age, Calculation Age, Empowering Age, then Farming Age... you get my point. Each, more difficult than the last. EACH, unlocking access to more powerful mods! EACH, making you want to quit more than the last! But, can you make it all the way to the final age? The Creative Age? I bet you not. Let's find out when we launch BlueShell MC: Season 2!
